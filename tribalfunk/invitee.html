JUDGE
ADDYCTION
Grooving all the way from Puri is our second judge,
Addyction, founder of the I.S.I.S crew. A core Hip Hop artist,
Addyction has been representing and
promoting the underground community of Bhubaneswar since 2014 and continues to do so.
Not only does he mesmerize everyone with his moves, but also with his songs,
which are a complete package of New Generation Hip Hop music and raps.
His hits include "Micha," "The Man is a Legend," "Reload," "Time Square," and "Juicy."
Addyction has represented our country in South Korea ,Nepal , Malaysia , and Dubai .
He is also the founder of Scene Sariba, an event that celebrates the culture of dance.

NEXTION
Catching and slashing all the beats brutally is the work of this artist,
the one who brings the most unexpected moves in the battles.
Nextion, our youngest judge and one of the Top 4 in the National Finals of Redbull DYS,

StreetBiz, ShowWhatYouGOT, Back to Disco, HouseInTheHouse,

The Matrix, and Dance Battle.

JUDGE
RAWSHAN
Popping straight from Kolkata,
we present our judge for the 1v1 RYS and 5v5 College Crew-On-Crew.
He is the winner of Bharat Jam vol 2 in “1v1 Popping” and “7 to Smoke Popping.”
He has also championed The Fam Jam “RYS 1v1,” Steez-O “7 to Smoke RYS,” and
the Redbull BC1 international jam “7 to Smoke” World Finals,
among many other titles.Representing our country in Poland , France , Malaysia ,
Canada , and Nepal , he holds the title of “National Popping Champion.”
He has judged numerous national jams, including Scene Sariba, The Culture, COP,
BOM, and will soon be judging at TRIBAL FUNK.
TRIBAL FUNK
DJ
ROSHAN
Music is the soul of the dancer,
and DJ Roshan embodies this sentiment.
A well- renowned DJ from Bhubaneswar, he began his career by creating
music specifically for dance battles in his hometown.
Over the years, DJ Roshan has made a significant mark in the dance scene by hosting
events such as Burnout, HouseInTheHouse, BackToDisco, Scene Sariba,
Breakbeats, War by Waves, and Alma Fiesta.
His expertise and dynamic presence as a DJ are essential to the success of these
dance battles, providing the perfect backdrop for dancers to showcase their skills.

AUDIOSLAVE
One of the fundamental pillars of Hip Hop is Emceeing,
where the host of the event plays a crucial role in engaging the audience.
Anirban Pal, also known as Audio Slave, exemplifies this role with
his exceptional talents as a beatboxer and emcee.
Renowned for his ability to captivate and connect with the crowd, Audio Slave
seamlessly blends his
impressive beatbox performances with light- hearted and entertaining commentary.
His unique approach to presenting event sponsors adds an extra layer

attentive, and thoroughly entertained throughout the event.
