DEMONS HOOD

Once a Demon, Always a Demon! Welcome to Demons Hood, a family forged by the Alumni of KIIT and former members of the legendary Do-U crew (Demons of Underground). We proudly represent the streets of Bhubaneswar, showcasing the raw, unfiltered art of underground battles. Our mission is to uplift and empower artists by providing them with a platform to express themselves and promoting the vibrant underground community of Bhubaneswar. From the proud parents of the Do-U Crew, we invite you to join us in this journey. Let’s come together, support each other, and pursue our passions, making us all true "Demons". Be a part of this dream and help us keep the spirit of underground art alive and thriving.
